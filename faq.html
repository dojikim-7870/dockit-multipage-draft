Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our PDF tools and services

1. What is Dokit.online?

Dokit.online is a free, online platform offering a suite of PDF tools including metadata editing, merging/dividing, compression, image conversion, security, and viewing. All tools are accessible directly from your browser without installing software.

2. How do I edit PDF metadata?

Simply go to the Metadata Editor, upload your PDF file, edit fields like Title, Author, Subject, Keywords, and then download the updated PDF.

3. Are there file size limits?

Yes, currently the maximum file size for uploads is 10MB for most tools. Larger files may need to be split or compressed first.

4. Is my PDF data secure?

All files are processed in your browser whenever possible. Uploaded files are temporarily handled and deleted automatically after processing to ensure your privacy and security.

5. Can I use Dokit.online for free?

Yes! All core PDF tools are completely free to use online without registration.

6. What formats are supported for conversion?

Our image conversion and other PDF tools support common formats like PDF, JPG, PNG, and TIFF. Specific tools may support additional formats.

7. Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed for most tools. Just upload your file, process it, and download the result.

8. Who can I contact for support?

For inquiries, suggestions, or issues, you can visit our Contact page to reach our support team.

9. Can I use Dokit.online on mobile devices?

Yes, our tools are fully responsive and work on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

10. How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

You can use our Contact page to report bugs, suggest improvements, or request new features. We value user feedback!
